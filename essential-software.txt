DBeaver
After installing with snap, need to apply a fix to prevent a couple of errors:
Edit the file: sudo nano /var/lib/snapd/desktop/applications/dbeaver-ce_dbeaver-ce.desktop
Find the line starting with: Exec=env BAMF_DESKTOP_FILE_HINT=...
Replace it entirely with: Exec=env BAMF_DESKTOP_FILE_HINT=/var/lib/snapd/desktop/applications/dbeaver-ce_dbeaver-ce.desktop NO_AT_BRIDGE=1 /snap/bin/dbeaver-ce
